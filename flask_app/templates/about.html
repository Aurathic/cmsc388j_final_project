General Description


A website for lost and found at UMD, where students can submit items they found on campus and other students can claim them. Students can make posts for items they have lost and also make posts for items they have found that may belong to someone. When there is a pair of posts for the same item, where one post is a post for lost item and the other is a post for found item, the 2 posts can then be linked together and are "resolved". Posts marked as resolved can appear in the user's post history, but will not appear in post searches. Anyone can sign up for an account, but UMD students that sign up with terpmail email have a special verification badge on their user profile.
